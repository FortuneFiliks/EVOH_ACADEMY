Academic Philosophy

Dr. Chijioke J. Evoh’s teaching integrates policy research, empirical analysis, and applied development practice. With a Ph.D. in Public & Urban Policy and decades of instructional experience across universities and public institutions, his academic engagement bridges theory, governance systems, and real-world policy implementation.

His classroom approach reflects:
  • Evidence-based inquiry
  • Interdisciplinary integration
  • Policy-to-practice application
  • Student-centered critical engagement
  • Global comparative perspective
